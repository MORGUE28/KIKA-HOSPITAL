Our Doctors

These are our great doctors we have for your care.

Dr. Rahul Kumar Singh

Orthopaedic surgeon(MD,mch)

Dr. Rahulkumar Singh is a Punjabi by birth. He received his medical degree from AIIMS in Delhi,where he attended college. He did his residency training at Mumbai's KEM Hospital. Dr. Rahul is particularly interested in the care of children and adolescents with medical problems. Dr. Rahul has ten years of ocular surgical experience, with a special interest in joint arthroscopy, bone fracture repair, and cataract surgery.

Dr. Rohini Mathur

General Physician(MBBS)

Rohini Mathur is a cardiothoracic surgeon from India. Dr. Deepti Chandrekar

Cargiologist(MD,FACC)

Deepti Chandrekar, MD, FACC, is an interventional cardiologist and the Eric & Sheila Samson Endowed Chair in Cardiovascular Health, director of disease management for Hoag Heart and Vascular Institute, and associate professor at the University of California, Irvine, School of Medicine. Dr. Asha Gupta

gynecologist(MD)

Dr. Asha Gupta provides comprehensive gynecologic care. Her practice is mostly referral based and emphasizes empathic, personalized, patient centered care.

Dr. Asha provides a range of surgical options from traditional vaginal surgery to a state of the art robotic surgery as well as newer options such as single-site and natural orifice surgery.
